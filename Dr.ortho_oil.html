Description:

Dr. Ortho Oil is an ayurvedic remedy designed to relieve various joint discomforts, including knee pain, back pain, elbow pain, neck pain, shoulder pain, and more. The oil is potent, offering relief from muscle discomfort and inflammation, with a smooth, non-sticky texture that absorbs easily into the skin.

Side effects:

  • Skin Irritation: Redness, itching, or rashes at the application site.

Massage 5 ml to 10 ml of oil onto the affected areas twice daily or as directed by a physician.
